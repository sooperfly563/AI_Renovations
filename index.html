C.O.N.C.R.E.T.E. Change

Creating Opportunities, Navigating Community Resilience, Empowerment,Training and Education Change purpose is to provide long term solutions for economic self-sufficiency and community empowerment. This is done by providing education and construction training opportunities for at-risk transitional age youth (12-24 years old) in nontraditional settings. Services will include leadership training, college prep counseling, job readiness, life skills development, mentoring, and Construction Trade Certifications.

MISSION:

Our mission is to positively affect the lives of as many youths as possible, by developing a solid foundation that all our youth can depend on for guidance and structure.

CONSTRUCTION TRAINING:

Hands-on Construction training for ages (12-24). Construction Training students are introduced to framing with wood/metal studs, drywalling with mud and tape, painting and introduced to the basic plumbing skills.

BUILD IT PROGRAM:

Offered to Middle and High School students, has open enrollment to encourage students to participate. The Build It program gives our youth an opportunity to be creative by building and designing useful objects.

The programs are offered during the traditional school day in after school hours. Students are enrolled in the offered available sessions and paired with mentors for the duration of the program. All programs offered are free of charge to interested students
